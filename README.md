EduCareer  is an intelligent career guidance web application designed to help users choose the most suitable career path based on multiple personal factors. Instead of relying only on marks or random suggestions, this platform analyzes a user’s academic performance, hobbies, mental interests, skills, and learning capacity to provide personalized career recommendations.

The website understands that every person is different. Some students may excel in academics, while others may have strong creative talents, practical skills, or passion-driven interests. By combining these factors, the system recommends careers where users can grow, succeed, and stay motivated.

Users can complete a guided assessment that evaluates their strengths, interests, and abilities. Based on the results, the platform suggests career options such as engineering, design, business, medicine, sports, content creation, research, entrepreneurship, and many more.

The goal of this project is to help students and professionals make smarter career decisions with confidence and clarity.

Key Highlights:

Career suggestions based on academic results
Recommendations using hobbies & passion
Matches careers with mental interests
Evaluates learning speed and capacity
Personalized career roadmap
User-friendly and modern interface

Purpose:

To bridge the gap between talent and career choice by helping users discover careers that truly match their potential.
